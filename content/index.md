Welcome. This is a **frame** — the reading surface and visual style are in
place; the chapters, and the interactive elements woven through them, come
next.

The idea: a calm, book-like reading experience where understanding is built by
*doing*, not just reading. As you progress, you'll find things to manipulate —
sliders that reshape a graph, snippets you can run, questions that check your
intuition — surfaced quietly, in context, the moment they're useful.

Start with the sample chapter below to see the style and a first interactive
element in action.
